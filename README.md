# HR Analytics & Employee Attrition Dashboard
### Power BI | DAX | IBM HR Dataset

## Problem Statement
Employee attrition costs organisations significantly in 
rehiring and retraining. This dashboard analyses 1,480 
employee records to identify at-risk segments and enable 
data-driven retention decisions.

## Dashboard Pages
- **Overview** — Workforce composition KPIs
- **Attrition Deep Dive** — Who is leaving and why
- **Satisfaction & Wellness** — Engagement metrics
- **Compensation** — Salary and income analysis

## Key Insights
- 30.5% attrition among overtime workers — 3× higher
- Year 1 tenure spike: 59 departures (highest point)
- Low salary band (<5k): 21.7% attrition vs 3.8% for 15k+
- All 5 satisfaction scores below 3.0/4.0

## DAX Measures Created
- Attrition Rate, Attrition Count, Total Employees
- Overtime Attrition, Singles Attrition
- Freq Travel Risk, Entry Level Risk
- Income Spread (Level 5 ÷ Level 1 income)

## Chart Types Used (12 unique)
Donut, Pie, Column, Horizontal Bar, Stacked Bar,
Area Chart, Line Chart, Treemap, Waterfall,
Gauge, Funnel, Table with Conditional Formatting

## Tools
Power BI Desktop | DAX | Power Query | Excel

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Attrition Dataset — Kaggle (1,480 rows, 38 columns)
